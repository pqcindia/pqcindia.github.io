LION

panthera leo

Lions are apex predators known as the 'King of the Jungle,' though they primarily inhabit savannas and grasslands. Living in social groups called prides, they are the only cats that hunt cooperatively. Male lions are distinguished by their impressive manes, which serve as protection and a symbol of strength. Their populations have declined significantly, with fewer than 25,000 remaining in the wild due to habitat loss and human-wildlife conflict.
